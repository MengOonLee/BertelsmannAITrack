\maketitle

Discounted return at time step $t$:
\begin{equation}
G_{t} = \sum_{k=0} \gamma^{k} R_{t+k+1}, \qquad \gamma \in [0, 1]
\end{equation}

One-step dynamics:
\begin{equation}
p(s', r | s, a) = \mathbb{P}(S_{t+1}=s', R_{t+1}=r | S_{t}=s, A_{t}=a)
\end{equation}
